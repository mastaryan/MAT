Coach Matthew Rigsby

Assistant Coach Bryan Station HS | 14U Bluesox Head Coach

Coach Matthew RigsbyBegins his 17th season coaching at the high school and collegiate levels. He graduated from Powell County High School, and went on the play baseball for Pikeville College. He has spent time at Powell County, Lawrence County, Louisville Desales, Jeffersonville High School(IN), Madison Southern, Madison Central, and he also spent a season as the interim head coach for University of Pikeville. He recently started coaching at Bryan Station High School in Lexington.

Throughout multiple Regional, District, and Conference Championships, he has also taken 3 separate programs to state championship appearances, as well as 9 former players playing at the professional levels. In 2012, Coach Rigsby also took a 16u team to Italy as apart of “America’s Team,” sponsored by the ACIS.

Four years ago he founded “Mid-America Prospects, Inc.,” which is a nonprofit organization that caters to student-athletes and giving back to the community through sports. You may also see Coach Rigsby on the University of Kentucky campus working the baseball camps and showcases as a Senior Instructor.
